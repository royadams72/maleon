Company Secretarial – Services relating to legal services of an incorporated company.

Every incorporated company needs to ensure that they comply with its obligations under company law. Ma’Leon and Co can assist you in the following so that you focus on your business:

  • Forming companies
  • Update your annual return (now called ‘Confirmation Statement’)
  • File your annual accounts in the specified time
  • Keeping you up to date with any legal changes

There are penalties for filing your accounts late to Companies House and these can be found here.

Important: Annual Accounts are not to be confused with Annual Returns. An Annual Return (now called ‘Confirmation Statement’ from 30th June 2016) is a snapshot of information that Companies House holds about a company (not financial information). Both, however, are legally required to be filed to Companies House on an annual basis. If you are unsure which is due please and we will be more than willing to check for you.
